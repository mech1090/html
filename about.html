Q: How do I redeem? A:You can redeem with a minimum of 20,000 PC Optimum points. You can redeem at any time for groceries or other in-store rewards at the check-out of participating stores.
Q: How do I redeem my PC Optimum points for Free Rewards? With a minimum of 20,000 PC Optimum points, you are eligible to redeem your PC Optimum points for grocery rewards and just about anything else at participating stores across Canada. Just redeem in increments of 10,000 PC Optimum points thereafter right at the checkout. For example: If you have 37,000 PC Optimum points you are eligible to redeem your PC Optimum points for $30 in free grocery rewards (20,000 PC Optimum points minimum redemption, plus an increment of 10,000 PC Optimum points). The remaining 7,000 points will remain in your PC Optimum points account until you reach the 20,000 PC Optimum points redemption level again.
Q: Are all rewards available across Canada? A:In-store rewards are available nationally in Canada at participating stores where President's Choice products are sold, however, merchandise selection varies by store.
Q: What types of insurance are available? A:PC® home and auto insurance is available in Alberta, New Brunswick, Newfoundland & Labrador, Nova Scotia, Ontario and Prince Edward Island. PC Financial Travel Insurance To Go is not available in Quebec or New Brunswick. PC pet and travel insurance is available in all Canadian provinces.
